Dive into the Heart of Japanese Pop Culture!

Do you dream of immersing yourself in the vibrant and dynamic world of Japanese pop culture? Well check out this list of exclusive tours to japan that we discovered online!

This list is filled with unforgettable journey through the most iconic and exciting destinations that every anime, manga, and cosplay enthusiast must experience.

Japan Journeys

Japan Journeys offers a range of tours tailored for anime, manga and cosplay fans

Japan summer manga tours: The holiday of a lifetime for fans of manga, anime, and all aspects of Japanese pop culture! As well as visits to Akihabara and Nakano Broadway, they will take you to all sorts of fantastic anime/manga related places in Tokyo!

First up is visiting the coveted Ghibli Museum, before moving on to quirky Harajuku, where is the latest trends in fashion and food among Japanese youth. A trip to Japan wouldn’t be complete without seeing the infamous Shibuya Crossing and statue of Hachiko, the dog who waited for his owner every day at the station. After that, visit Ikebukuro, home to Japan’s largest Pokemon Centre and Animate store, Akihabara, and so much more! Experience Tokyo in all its neon glory with the original Japan Summer Manga Tour.
