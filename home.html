About Us

OtakuTokyo was founded in June 24th 2024 with a vision to create a central hub for anime, manga, and cosplay enthusiasts. Our passion for Japanese culture and entertainment drives us to provide the best resources and community experiences for fans around the world.

Our Story

We have grown into a thriving online community. Our journey has been fueled by a deep love for anime and manga, and a desire to bring fans closer together.

We specialize in bringing the latest and greatest from the world of anime and manga. Our offerings include:

Anime Reviews and Recommendations: Get insights on the best series to watch.

Manga News and Updates: Stay informed on upcoming releases and must-read titles.

Cosplay Resources: Find everything you need for your next cosplay project, from tutorials to supplies.

We are proudly based in United Kingdom, but our community spans the globe. Connect with us online and join events, discussions, and more, no matter where you are.
